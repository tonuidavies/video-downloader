# Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 10, 2026

SaveIt All ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the SaveIt All mobile application.

## 1. Information We Process

### Information you provide
- Video links you paste into the app for analysis and download.

### Information stored on your device
- Download history and metadata (for example: title, thumbnail, local file path) stored locally on your device using app storage.
- Downloaded media files saved to your device storage.

### Automatically processed information
- Basic technical and device information may be processed by third-party services used by the app (for example, advertising and analytics providers).

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:
- Analyze links and retrieve downloadable media information.
- Download and save media to your device when you request it.
- Show your saved items in your in-app library.
- Display rewarded ads and maintain app functionality.
- Improve app stability, performance, and security.

## 3. Advertising

SaveIt All uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display rewarded ads. AdMob and its partners may process device and usage data to provide, measure, and improve ads.

Learn more:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from apps: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

## 4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services, including:
- Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)
- Remote media extraction/downloader API used by the app

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited technical data with service providers only as needed to operate the app (for example, ad delivery and link processing).

## 6. Data Retention

- Data stored locally in the app remains on your device until you remove the app data or uninstall the app.
- Third-party providers retain data according to their own policies.

## 7. Children

This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8. Your Choices

You can:
- Stop using the app at any time.
- Clear local app data from your device settings.
- Uninstall the app to remove local storage data.

## 9. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect data processed by the app. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

## 10. International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own by third-party providers.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised effective date.
